❯ Meet Dr. Khaled Elsherbiny

PLASTIC SURGEON

Professor Khaled Elsherbiny

  • Doctoral degree in Plastic, Burn, and Maxillofacial Surgery from Ain Shams University, Cairo.
  • Master’s degree in Plastic Surgery from Ain Shams University, Cairo.
  • Specialized training in cosmetic surgery through basic and advanced courses at UT Southwestern University, USA.

  • Joined the Plastic Surgery Department at Ain Shams University in 1994 as a specialist.
  • Promoted to Associate Professor, then Professor of Plastic Surgery at Ain Shams University.
  • Tutored undergraduate students and plastic surgery specialists in basic and advanced courses.
  • Conducted specialized training programs, with a focus on rhinoplasty.

Dr. Khaled Elsherbiny is a globally respected plastic surgeon with over three decades of experience in aesthetic and reconstructive surgery. Beginning his career at Ain Shams University in 1994, he gained extensive expertise in burns, maxillofacial surgery, and aesthetic procedures like breast surgery, body contouring, and rhinoplasty. As an international visiting plastic surgeon in the Gulf region, he has built a reputation for excellence. Currently, Dr. Khaled serves as a plastic surgery consultant in the UAE and Egypt, delivering personalized, transformative results with a commitment to enhancing natural beauty.
